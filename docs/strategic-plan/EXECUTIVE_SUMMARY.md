## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Brand Transformation: Dumuwaks to FundiPro

**Recommendation:** Rebrand to "FundiPro"

| Criterion | FundiPro Score |
|-----------|---------------|
| Service Clarity | 10/10 - "Fundi" signals technician services |
| Professional Appeal | 9/10 - "Pro" elevates perception |
| Memorability | 9/10 - Simple, 3 syllables |
| Local Authenticity | 10/10 - Swahili term universal in Kenya |
| Regional Scalability | 7/10 - Works across East Africa |

**Tagline:** "Skilled Hands. Trusted Service."

### 2. Service Discovery Innovation: WORD BANK

**Problem Solved:** Users struggle to describe technical problems and find appropriate services.

**Solution:** Visual, tappable service selection interface with 99+ services across 12 categories.

| Benefit | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Service discovery time | From 5+ minutes to <1 minute |
| Taps to find service | From 8+ to 3 |
| Customer satisfaction | Target 4.7/5 |

### 3. Technician Empowerment: Custom Services & Pricing

**Problem Solved:** Technicians limited to predefined categories; cannot differentiate.

**Solution:**
- Technicians add custom services to the platform
- Set own pricing (hourly, fixed, per-unit, milestone)
- Define payment preferences and payout schedules
- Build portfolio galleries to showcase expertise

### 4. Payment Security: Escrow + Flexible Plans

**Problem Solved:** Payment disputes, no-shows, price surprises.

**Solution:**
- M-Pesa STK Push for seamless payments
- Escrow holding until service confirmation
- Milestone payments for large jobs (KES 30,000+)
- Tiered commission: 20%/15%/12%/10% by transaction value
