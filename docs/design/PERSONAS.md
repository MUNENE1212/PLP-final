# Dumuwaks User Personas

## Overview

This document defines the three primary user personas for Dumuwaks, a technician services marketplace in Kenya. Each persona is designed to represent real user needs, behaviors, pain points, and contexts of use.

---

## Persona 1: Customer - Wanjiku Muthoni

### Demographics
- **Name**: Wanjiku Muthoni
- **Age**: 34 years old
- **Occupation**: Small business owner (bakery in Westlands, Nairobi)
- **Technical Literacy**: Moderate - uses smartphone daily, comfortable with M-Pesa, WhatsApp, Facebook
- **Primary Device**: Samsung Galaxy A series (6.5" screen)
- **Context of Use**: Busy, often multitasking, needs quick solutions during work hours

### Goals and Motivations
1. **Primary Goal**: Find reliable, trustworthy technicians quickly for both home and business needs
2. **Save Time**: Does not want to spend hours calling different technicians or waiting for callbacks
3. **Quality Assurance**: Needs assurance that the technician will do quality work
4. **Fair Pricing**: Wants transparent pricing without hidden fees or negotiation hassles
5. **Trust**: Needs to feel safe inviting technicians to her home/business

### Key Actions to Perform
| Action | Frequency | Priority |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Search for technicians by service type | 2-3x/month | HIGH |
| View technician profiles and ratings | 2-3x/month | HIGH |
| Book a technician for specific date/time | 2-3x/month | HIGH |
| Pay booking fee via M-Pesa | 2-3x/month | HIGH |
| Track technician arrival | As needed | MEDIUM |
| Rate and review completed services | After each job | MEDIUM |
| Message technician about job details | As needed | MEDIUM |

### Information Needs at Each Step

#### Service Discovery
- What services are available?
- Which technicians are near me?
- What does each service typically cost?
- How quickly can someone come?

#### Technician Selection
- Is this technician verified/trustworthy?
- What do other customers say?
- What is their experience level?
- What is their hourly rate?
- Are they available now?

#### Booking
- What is the total estimated cost?
- What is the booking fee?
- When can they come?
- What information do I need to provide?

#### During Service
- When will the technician arrive?
- How do I contact them?
- What if something goes wrong?

#### Post-Service
- Was the job completed satisfactorily?
- How do I rate/review?
- How do I pay the remaining balance?

### Pain Points and Frustrations
1. **Trust Issues**: "How do I know this technician won't steal from me or damage my property?"
2. **Price Uncertainty**: "I hate not knowing the final price until the job is done"
3. **Reliability**: "Technicians often don't show up on time or at all"
4. **Quality Concerns**: "Hard to verify skills before hiring"
5. **Communication Barriers**: "Difficult to describe technical problems over phone"
6. **Payment Friction**: "Negotiating prices is uncomfortable"
7. **No Follow-up**: "No recourse if job is poorly done"

### Contextual Factors
- Often has poor network connectivity
- Prefers Swahili/English mix
- May have limited data bundle
- Usually in a hurry (running business)
- Often needs services urgently (emergencies)

## Persona 2: Technician - John Kamau

### Demographics
- **Name**: John Kamau
- **Age**: 42 years old
- **Occupation**: Electrician with 12 years experience
- **Technical Literacy**: Basic-Moderate - uses smartphone for calls, WhatsApp, M-Pesa
- **Primary Device**: Tecno Spark (6.0" screen)
- **Context of Use**: On the move, at job sites, often in areas with poor connectivity

### Goals and Motivations
1. **Primary Goal**: Get consistent, well-paying jobs to support his family
2. **Build Reputation**: Establish himself as a trusted, quality technician
3. **Efficient Workflow**: Minimize time spent finding clients
4. **Fair Compensation**: Get paid promptly and fairly for work done
5. **Professional Growth**: Expand skills and client base

### Key Actions to Perform
| Action | Frequency | Priority |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Create and update profile | Once + updates | HIGH |
| Set availability status | Daily | HIGH |
| Define services and pricing | Initial + updates | HIGH |
| Accept/decline job requests | Multiple daily | HIGH |
| View job details and location | Multiple daily | HIGH |
| Navigate to customer location | Multiple daily | HIGH |
| Update job status (arrived, started, completed) | Per job | HIGH |
| Request payment | Per job | HIGH |
| Upload work photos for portfolio | After jobs | MEDIUM |
| Respond to customer messages | Throughout day | MEDIUM |

### Information Needs at Each Step

#### Profile Setup
- What information do I need to provide?
- How do I prove my skills/experience?
- What photos should I upload?
- How do I set my pricing?

#### Receiving Jobs
- What is the job description?
- Where is the customer located?
- When do they need me?
- What is the estimated pay?

#### During Job
- How do I contact the customer?
- What if I can't find the location?
- How do I update the customer on progress?

#### Payment
- How and when will I get paid?
- What if customer refuses to pay?
- How do I handle disputes?

### Pain Points and Frustrations
1. **Inconsistent Work**: "Some weeks I have too many jobs, others none"
2. **Customer No-Shows**: "Waste time and money going to jobs that get cancelled"
3. **Payment Delays**: "Customers delay paying or refuse to pay full amount"
4. **Price Negotiations**: "Customers always want to negotiate down"
5. **Unclear Job Descriptions**: "Arrive at jobs that are different from described"
6. **Travel Costs**: "Spend money on transport to jobs that don't pay well"
7. **Lack of Recognition**: "Hard to stand out from less skilled technicians"

### Contextual Factors
- Limited data bundle - prefers offline-capable apps
- Often in areas with poor network
- May not have bank account (uses M-Pesa)
- Relies on word-of-mouth for trust
- Values quick, simple interfaces
- May share phone with family members

## Persona 3: Admin - Amina Ochieng

### Demographics
- **Name**: Amina Ochieng
- **Age**: 28 years old
- **Occupation**: Platform Operations Manager
- **Technical Literacy**: High - comfortable with complex interfaces, analytics tools
- **Primary Device**: Laptop (15" screen) + iPhone (6.1" screen)
- **Context of Use**: Office environment, needs to respond to issues quickly

### Goals and Motivations
1. **Primary Goal**: Ensure platform runs smoothly and users are satisfied
2. **Quality Control**: Verify technicians and maintain service quality
3. **Dispute Resolution**: Resolve conflicts between customers and technicians fairly
4. **Growth**: Grow user base and transaction volume
5. **Insights**: Understand platform performance and identify improvements

### Key Actions to Perform
| Action | Frequency | Priority |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Review technician applications | Daily | HIGH |
| Verify technician credentials | As submitted | HIGH |
| Handle user complaints/disputes | As needed | HIGH |
| Monitor platform metrics | Daily | HIGH |
| Manage pricing/service categories | As needed | MEDIUM |
| Generate reports | Weekly/monthly | MEDIUM |
| Communicate policy changes | As needed | MEDIUM |
| Support users with issues | Throughout day | HIGH |

### Information Needs at Each Step

#### Technician Verification
- What credentials has the technician submitted?
- How do I verify their identity?
- What is their background check status?

#### Dispute Resolution
- What happened from both perspectives?
- What is the booking/payment history?
- What evidence is available (photos, messages)?

#### Platform Monitoring
- How many active bookings?
- What is the booking success rate?
- Are there any flagged issues?
- What is user satisfaction score?

### Pain Points and Frustrations
1. **Verification Challenges**: "Hard to verify technician credentials in Kenya"
2. **Dispute He said/She said**: "Difficult to determine truth in disputes"
3. **Manual Processes**: "Too many things require manual intervention"
4. **User Education**: "Users don't understand how to use features"
5. **Fraud Prevention**: "Constant battle against fake accounts and scams"

### Contextual Factors
- Needs comprehensive dashboards
- Requires mobile access for urgent issues
- Works with support team
- Needs to make quick decisions
- Documents everything for compliance

## Design Implications

### For Customers (Wanjiku)
1. **Mobile-first design** with offline capability
2. **Large touch targets** (56px minimum for primary actions)
3. **Trust signals prominently displayed** (verification badges, ratings, reviews)
4. **Simple, visual service selection** (WORD BANK concept)
5. **Transparent pricing** upfront with no hidden fees
6. **Quick booking flow** (5 taps maximum to confirm booking)
7. **Real-time updates** via WhatsApp/SMS notifications

### For Technicians (John)
1. **Low-data mode** with essential offline features
2. **Simple, large interface elements**
3. **Quick availability toggle**
4. **Clear job cards** with all essential info visible
5. **One-tap navigation** to customer location
6. **Easy payment request** workflow
7. **Photo upload** for work portfolio (simplified)

### For Admins (Amina)
1. **Comprehensive dashboard** with real-time metrics
2. **Bulk action capabilities**
3. **Detailed audit trails**
4. **Quick decision support** with relevant context
5. **Mobile-responsive** for urgent issues
6. **Automated alerts** for critical issues

## User Story Mapping

### Customer Journey Stories
```
AS Wanjiku, I WANT TO:
1. Quickly find the service I need (using visual service selection)
2. See technicians near me with ratings and prices
3. Book a technician in under 2 minutes
4. Know exactly what I'll pay before booking
5. Track when my technician will arrive
6. Pay easily via M-Pesa
7. Rate my experience to help others
```

### Technician Journey Stories
```
AS John, I WANT TO:
1. Set up my profile easily with my skills
2. Define my own services and prices
3. Toggle my availability with one tap
4. See job requests with clear details and pay
5. Navigate to customers easily
6. Get paid quickly after completing jobs
7. Build my reputation with reviews and photos
```

### Admin Journey Stories
```
AS Amina, I WANT TO:
1. See platform health at a glance
2. Quickly verify or reject technician applications
3. Access all context when resolving disputes
4. Identify and act on suspicious activity
5. Understand user behavior through analytics
6. Communicate with users efficiently
7. Maintain quality standards across the platform
```
