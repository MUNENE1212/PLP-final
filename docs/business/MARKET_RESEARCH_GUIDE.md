## Research Objectives

### Primary Objectives

**1. Validate Market Size**
- What is the TRUE size of Kenya's maintenance/repair market?
- How much do households actually spend monthly on maintenance/repairs?
- What is the addressable market for Dumu Waks in Nairobi and Kenya?

**2. Validate Customer Pain Points**
- What are the TOP 3 problems customers face finding technicians?
- How do customers currently find technicians? (actual behavior, not what they say)
- What would make customers switch to a digital platform?
- How much are customers willing to pay for convenience/quality assurance?

**3. Validate Technician Pain Points**
- What is technicians' ACTUAL monthly income? (median, not just top performers)
- What are the TOP 3 problems technicians face finding work?
- How much time do technicians spend on unpaid activities (finding customers, negotiating)?
- What would make technicians join a digital platform?

**4. Validate Pricing & Unit Economics**
- What is the AVERAGE booking value? (real data from platform)
- What is customers' price sensitivity for premium features?
- What is technicians' willingness to pay for platform access?
- What is the REAL CAC by channel?

**5. Validate Competitive Positioning**
- Who are the ACTUAL competitors (direct and indirect)?
- What market share do competitors currently have?
- What are competitors' strengths and weaknesses?
- What is Dumu Waks' true differentiation?

## Primary Research Methods

### Method 1: Online Surveys

**Target Sample Sizes:**
- **Customers:** 500+ homeowners in Nairobi
- **Technicians:** 200+ skilled tradespeople in Nairobi
- **Statistical Validity:** 95% confidence level, ±5% margin of error

**Survey Distribution Channels:**

**For Customers:**
1. **Facebook Ads:**
   - Targeting: Nairobi, 25-55 years old, homeowners
   - Budget: 20,000-30,000 KES
   - Incentive: Enter drawing for 5,000 KES home service credit
   - Expected response: 300-400 completions

2. **Google Surveys:**
   - Target: Nairobi population
   - Cost: ~100-200 KES per complete
   - Expected: 100-150 completions

3. **Email Lists:**
   - If Dumu Waks has customer email list
   - Send survey to existing customers
   - Incentive: 10% off next booking
   - Expected: 50-100 completions

**For Technicians:**
1. **Facebook Groups:**
   - Join "Kenya Plumbers Association", "Electricians Kenya", etc.
   - Post survey with incentive
   - Incentive: 500 KES M-Pesa for completion
   - Expected: 100-150 completions

2. **On-the-Ground Recruitment:**
   - Visit hardware stores, construction sites
   - Tablet-based surveys
   - Immediate incentive: 200 KES airtime
   - Expected: 50-100 completions

**Survey Duration:**
- Customer survey: 8-10 minutes
- Technician survey: 12-15 minutes
- Keep surveys SHORT to maximize completion

## Survey Design

### Customer Survey Template

**Section 1: Screening & Demographics (2 minutes)**

1. Do you own or rent your home? [Own/Rent]
2. How long have you lived at your current address? [Less than 6 months / 6 months-2 years / 2-5 years / 5+ years]
3. Which neighborhood in Nairobi do you live in? [Dropdown list]
4. What is your approximate household income per month? [Ranges]
5. How many people live in your household? [Number]

**Section 2: Current Behavior (3 minutes)**

6. In the past 6 months, how many times have you hired a technician for home repairs/maintenance? [Number]
7. What types of services did you hire for? [Select all: Plumbing, Electrical, Carpentry, Painting, HVAC, Welding, Masonry, Other]
8. How did you find the technician? [Select all: Word-of-mouth / Facebook / Jiji / Google Search / Hardware Store Referral / Already knew them / Other]
9. How long did it take to find a technician? [Less than 1 hour / 1-4 hours / 4-24 hours / 1-3 days / More than 3 days]
10. Did the technician show up on time? [Yes / No / Partially]
11. Were you satisfied with the quality of work? [Very Satisfied / Satisfied / Neutral / Dissatisfied / Very Dissatisfied]
12. How much did you pay for the last service? [Amount in KES]

**Section 3: Pain Points (2 minutes)**

13. What was the MOST frustrating part of hiring a technician? [Rank top 3: Finding someone available / Uncertainty about quality / Price transparency / Waiting for technician to arrive / Payment issues / Poor quality work / Technician didn't show / Other]
14. Have you ever had a BAD experience with a technician? [Yes / No]
15. If yes, what happened? [Open text]
16. On a scale of 1-10, how stressful is it to find a reliable technician? [1-10]

**Section 4: Value Proposition (2 minutes)**

17. How much MORE would you pay for a verified technician with 4.5+ rating vs unverified? [% premium: 0% / 5% / 10% / 15% / 20%+]
18. Would you use a platform that shows you EXACT pricing before booking? [Yes / No / Maybe]
19. Would you use a platform that holds your payment in escrow until you're satisfied? [Yes / No / Maybe]
20. Would you use a platform that uses AI to match you with the RIGHT technician for your specific problem? [Yes / No / Maybe]
21. What features would make you switch from your current method? [Rank: Verified quality / Transparent pricing / Instant matching / Secure payments / Real-time tracking / All of the above]

**Section 5: Platform Awareness & Usage (1 minute)**

22. Have you heard of Dumu Waks? [Yes / No]
23. Have you used Dumu Waks? [Yes / No]
24. If yes, how was your experience? [Very Satisfied / Satisfied / Neutral / Dissatisfied / Very Dissatisfied]
25. Would you recommend Dumu Waks to a friend? [Yes / No / Maybe]
26. What would make you use Dumu Waks again? [Open text]

**Incentive Delivery:**
- Collect phone number or email
- Send M-Pesa airtime or enter drawing
- Ensure privacy: "Your responses will be kept confidential"

### Technician Survey Template

**Section 1: Screening & Demographics (2 minutes)**

1. What is your primary trade/skill? [Dropdown: Plumbing, Electrical, Carpentry, Painting, HVAC, Welding, Masonry, Other]
2. How many years of experience do you have? [Number]
3. Do you have formal training/certification in your trade? [Yes / No]
4. What neighborhoods in Nairobi do you primarily work in? [Select all]
5. Do you have a smartphone? [Yes / No]
6. Do you use M-Pesa? [Yes / No]

**Section 2: Current Work & Income (4 minutes)**

7. In a typical week, how many jobs do you complete? [Number]
8. In a typical month, what is your TOTAL income from all jobs? [Amount in KES]
9. What was your BEST month income in the last 6 months? [Amount in KES]
10. What was your WORST month income in the last 6 months? [Amount in KES]
11. How many days per week do you actively look for work? [Number]
12. How many HOURS per day do you spend finding customers (calling, posting, asking for referrals)? [Number]
13. How do you currently find most of your customers? [Rank: Word-of-mouth / Facebook / Jiji / Hardware store referrals / Repeat customers / Other]
14. What percentage of your income comes from repeat customers? [%]
15. How often do you have to negotiate your price down? [Always / Often / Sometimes / Rarely / Never]

**Section 3: Pain Points (3 minutes)**

16. What are your TOP 3 challenges as a technician? [Rank: Finding enough work / Getting paid fair prices / Getting paid on time / Building reputation / Finding new customers / Dealing with difficult customers / Other]
17. Have you ever completed a job and NOT gotten paid? [Yes / No]
18. If yes, how much money did you lose in the past 6 months from unpaid jobs? [Amount in KES]
19. Have you ever had a customer cancel or postpone at the last minute? [Yes / No]
20. How often does this happen? [Often / Sometimes / Rarely]
21. On a scale of 1-10, how stressful is your income insecurity? [1-10]

**Section 4: Value Proposition (3 minutes)**

22. Would you join a platform that sends you 5-10 job requests every day? [Yes / No / Maybe]
23. What percentage of your earnings would you be willing to pay for this service? [0% / 5% / 10% / 15% / 20%+]
24. Would you join a platform that guarantees payment within 24 hours? [Yes / No / Maybe]
25. Would you join a platform that helps you build a verified reputation that travels with you? [Yes / No / Maybe]
26. Would you join a platform that offers tier progression so your income grows as your reputation grows? [Yes / No / Maybe]
27. What would make you switch from your current methods to a digital platform? [Rank: More consistent work / Higher prices / Guaranteed payment / Portable reputation / Less time finding customers / All of the above]

**Section 5: Platform Awareness & Usage (1 minute)**

28. Have you heard of Dumu Waks? [Yes / No]
29. Have you used Dumu Waks as a technician? [Yes / No]
30. If yes, how was your experience? [Very Satisfied / Satisfied / Neutral / Dissatisfied / Very Dissatisfied]
31. Would you recommend Dumu Waks to other technicians? [Yes / No / Maybe]
32. What would make you use Dumu Waks more? [Open text]

**Incentive Delivery:**
- Collect M-Pesa number
- Send 500 KES via M-Pesa upon completion
- Ensure privacy: "Your responses will be kept confidential"

## Interview Guides

### Customer Interview Guide

**Target:** 20 in-depth interviews (30-45 minutes each)

**Recruitment:**
- 10 customers who have used Dumu Waks
- 10 customers who have NOT used Dumu Waks
- Offer incentive: 1,000 KES home service credit

**Interview Questions:**

**Opening (5 minutes):**
1. Tell me about yourself and your household.
2. How long have you lived in your current home?
3. Do you own or rent?

**Current Behavior (10 minutes):**
4. Walk me through the LAST time you needed to hire a technician.
   - What was the problem?
   - How did you find them?
   - How long did it take?
   - What was the experience like?
5. How much did you pay? Was it clear upfront or did it change?
6. Were you satisfied with the work? Why or why not?
7. Have you hired them again? Why or why not?

**Pain Points (10 minutes):**
8. Tell me about a BAD experience you've had with a technician.
   - What happened?
   - How did it make you feel?
   - What did you do?
9. What is the MOST frustrating part of hiring technicians for you?
10. How do you currently VERIFY that a technician is qualified?
11. How do you know if the PRICE is fair?
12. Have you ever felt UNSAFE with a technician in your home?

**Dumu Waks Experience (10 minutes)** [If they've used it]:
13. How did you hear about Dumu Waks?
14. Walk me through your booking experience.
15. What did you LIKE about it?
16. What did you DISLIKE about it?
17. How did it compare to your previous method of finding technicians?
18. Would you use it again? Why or why not?
19. Would you recommend it to friends? Why or why not?

**Dumu Waks Concept (10 minutes)** [If they haven't used it]:
20. [Show Dumu Waks platform demo]
21. What is your INITIAL REACTION?
22. What features are MOST appealing to you?
23. What features are LEAST appealing or confusing?
24. What would make you TRY it?
25. What would make you NOT try it?
26. How much would you be willing to pay for this service?

**Closing (5 minutes):**
27. Is there anything else about hiring technicians that I should know?
28. If you could change ONE THING about how you currently find technicians, what would it be?
29. Thank you! [Incentive delivery info]

**Recording:**
- Ask permission to record (audio or video)
- Take notes during interview
- Send thank you note with incentive info

## Competitive Intelligence

### Methodology

**1. Competitor Identification**
- Direct: Jiji, Biashara.co, any other service marketplaces
- Indirect: Hardware stores, property managers, real estate agents
- International: Uber (if offering services in Kenya), TaskRabbit (if in Kenya)

**2. Data Collection**

**For Each Competitor:**
- Total service provider listings (count manually or estimate)
- Pricing structure (free listing? commission? featured listing?)
- Trust features (reviews, verification, escrow)
- User experience (sign up as customer, document flow)
- Mobile app availability (check app stores)
- Social media presence (followers, engagement)
- Funding status (Crunchbase, news articles)
- Launch date (Wayback Machine)

**3. Mystery Shopping**
- Sign up as customer
- Post a job request
- Document response time
- Document quality of responses
- Document pricing transparency
- Document payment process

**4. Customer Reviews Analysis**
- Read 100+ reviews of competitors
- Identify common complaints
- Identify common praises
- Note gaps in market

## Data Analysis & Validation

### Quantitative Analysis

**1. Descriptive Statistics**
- Means, medians, modes
- Standard deviations
- Frequency distributions

**2. Cross-Tabulation**
- Customer behavior by demographics
- Technician income by experience level
- Pain points by customer segment

**3. Statistical Significance**
- Chi-square tests for categorical data
- T-tests for continuous data
- 95% confidence intervals

**4. Correlation Analysis**
- Income vs satisfaction
- Age vs technology adoption
- Experience level vs income

### Qualitative Analysis

**1. Thematic Analysis**
- Code interview transcripts
- Identify recurring themes
- Quote extraction

**2. Sentiment Analysis**
- Positive/negative/neutral sentiment
- Pain point frequency
- Delight feature frequency

**3. Persona Development**
- Create customer personas based on research
- Create technician personas based on research

### Validation

**1. Triangulation**
- Compare survey data with interview data
- Compare primary research with secondary research
- Compare Dumu Waks internal data with external benchmarks

**2. Sanity Checks**
- Do market size estimates align with known data?
- Do income estimates align with cost of living?
- Do growth rates align with industry benchmarks?

**3. Peer Review**
- Have team members review analysis
- Have external advisors review findings
- Challenge assumptions
