## Executive Summary

Healthy marketplace dynamics require **balanced supply and demand**. For every 1,000 customers, you need approximately 200-250 active technicians to ensure:
- <60 second matching time
- 90%+ customer satisfaction
- Technician income stability (5-10 jobs/week)

**Target Metrics:**
- 250 new technicians in 90 days
- 80% activation rate (200 active technicians)
- Average 5+ bookings/week per technician
- 4.5+ star average rating
- 75%+ 30-day retention

**Budget:** 350,000 KES for 90 days
