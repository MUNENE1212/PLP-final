# Dumu Waks - Customer Acquisition Strategy
## Practical Marketing Plan for the Kenyan Market

**Date:** January 9, 2026
**Status:** Ready for Execution
**Target:** Acquire 1,000 active customers in 90 days

---

## Executive Summary

This plan focuses on **cost-effective, high-ROI customer acquisition** tactics proven to work in the Kenyan market. We'll prioritize channels with lowest CAC and highest retention, track everything from day 1, and iterate based on real data.

**Target Metrics:**
- 1,000 new customers in 90 days
- Average CAC: ≤300 KES per customer
- 30-day retention: ≥40%
- Booking completion rate: ≥70%

**Budget Allocation:**
- Total: 300,000 KES for 90 days
- Channels: Referral program, Facebook ads, Content marketing, Partnerships

## Phase 2: Channel Strategy (Week 3-12)

### Channel 1: Referral Program (Priority #1)

**Why Lowest CAC:**
- Trust-based recommendations convert best
- Highest retention rates
- Viral coefficient >1 possible

**Incentive Structure:**
```
Referrer (existing customer):
- 500 KES credit for every successful referral
- Maximum 5 referrals per month (2,500 KES)
- Credit applied to next booking

Referee (new customer):
- 10% off first booking (max 1,000 KES discount)
- Must complete and pay for booking
- Referrer gets credit after referee's booking completes
```

**A. Referral Code System**
