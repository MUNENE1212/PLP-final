# Dumuwaks Strategic Transformation Plan

## Executive Summary

This strategic transformation plan outlines a comprehensive repositioning of Dumuwaks to target the entire Kenyan technician services industry. The plan encompasses brand transformation, service model innovation, technician profile enhancement, and market strategy.

## Key Strategic Decisions

### 1. Brand Transformation

**Decision**: Recommend rebranding from "Dumu Waks" to "FundiPro"

**Rationale**:
- Current brand name lacks clarity and industry relevance
- "Fundi" is universally understood in Kenya for technician/artisan services
- "Pro" suffix adds professional credibility
- Better positioning for industry-wide appeal

**See**: [BRAND_STRATEGY.md](./BRAND_STRATEGY.md) Section 2.2

### 2. Service Model Innovation

**Decision**: Implement "WORD BANK" service discovery system

**Rationale**:
- Simplifies service selection for customers
- Enables technicians to define custom services
- Visual, intuitive interface for 99+ service types
- Differentiates from competitors with rigid service lists

**See**: [SERVICE_MODEL_DESIGN.md](./SERVICE_MODEL_DESIGN.md) Part 1-2

### 3. Technician Payment Flexibility

**Decision**: Allow technicians to define their own payment plans

**Rationale**:
- Different technicians have different business models
- Flexibility attracts diverse technician base
- Supports hourly, fixed, milestone, and custom pricing

**See**: [TECHNICIAN_PROFILE_BLUEPRINT.md](./TECHNICIAN_PROFILE_BLUEPRINT.md) Part 4

### 4. Market Focus

**Decision**: Start with Nairobi, phase geographic expansion

**Rationale**:
- Highest density of potential customers
- Best digital infrastructure
- Concentrated marketing efforts
- Prove model before expansion

**See**: [INDUSTRY_MARKET_ANALYSIS.md](./INDUSTRY_MARKET_ANALYSIS.md) Part 7
