# Dumu Waks - Value Proposition Analysis

**Date:** December 28, 2025
**Framework:** Jobs-to-be-Done + Value Proposition Canvas

---

## Executive Summary

Dumu Waks creates value by transferring three core advantages to its users:
1. **Trust Infrastructure:** Verified reputation, escrow payments, review systems
2. **Efficiency Gains:** AI matching reduces search friction, increases successful connections
3. **Economic Optimization:** Better technician earnings, fair customer pricing, transparent platform fees

---

## Value Proposition Canvas - Customer Segment

### Customer Profile

#### Jobs-to-be-Done

**Functional Jobs:**
1. **Find and hire qualified technicians** for specific maintenance/repair needs
2. **Get work completed** to acceptable quality standards
3. **Pay fair prices** with transparent pricing breakdown
4. **Complete transactions securely** without risk of fraud or poor quality

**Social Jobs:**
1. **Maintain home/business appearance** to avoid social embarrassment
2. **Demonstrate responsibility** as a homeowner/business owner
3. **Access reliable help** to show competence in managing property

**Emotional Jobs:**
1. **Feel secure** that qualified professionals are working on their property
2. **Reduce anxiety** about unexpected costs or poor quality work
3. **Gain peace of mind** that problems will be resolved correctly
4. **Avoid frustration** of unreliable service providers

#### Pains

**Before Using Dumu Waks:**
- **Search Friction:** "I called 7 plumbers, none answered, 2 said they'd come but didn't show up"
- **Quality Uncertainty:** "I don't know if this person is actually qualified or if they'll burn my house down"
- **Price Opacity:** "They quoted 3,000 KES but then charged 12,000 KES with 'materials' and 'transport'"
- **Time Waste:** "I waited all day Saturday, and they never showed"
- **Payment Risk:** "If I pay upfront, what if they don't finish? If I pay after, what if they demand more?"
- **No Recourse:** "They did terrible work, left a mess, and I have no way to get it fixed"
- **Emergency Panic:** "It's 10pm and my basement is flooding - who can I trust to come now?"

**Severity of Pains:**
- **High Severity:** Emergency situations, safety risks (electrical, structural), costly damage
- **Medium Severity:** Inconvenience, lost time, wasted money
- **Low Severity:** Annoyance, minor quality issues

#### Gains

**Expected Gains:**
- Find technician quickly (within hours, not days)
- Know technician qualifications before hiring
- Get clear price estimate upfront
- Have work completed on schedule
- Pay securely after satisfactory completion
- Leave review to help others

**Unexpected Gains (Delighters):**

- **Quality Verification:** "I can see 47 reviews, 4.8-star rating, photos of their past work"
- **Transparent Pricing:** "I can see EXACTLY why it costs 5,200 KES - base price + distance + urgency + time + technician tier"
- **Emergency Speed:** "I posted at 10pm, had a verified plumber at my door by 11:30pm"
- **Portable Trust:** "I moved from Mombasa to Nairobi, but I can still use the same trusted platform"
- **Dispute Protection:** "The work wasn't great, and Dumu Waks mediated and got me a partial refund"
- **Repeat Convenience:** "I just click 'Rebook with John' - all my details are saved"

**Outcomes Matter More Than Features:**
- **Feature:** "99+ service types"
- **Outcome:** "Whatever breaks, I can find help in one place"

- **Feature:** "AI matching algorithm"
