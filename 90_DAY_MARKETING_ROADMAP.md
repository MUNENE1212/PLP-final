## Executive Summary

This 90-day plan takes a **sustainable, data-driven approach** to growing Dumu Waks from your current base to 1,000 active customers. Instead of rushing and burning cash, we'll build systematically, learn what works, and double down on winning strategies.

**The Philosophy:**
- Month 1: Test, Learn, Validate (0-200 customers)
- Month 2: Optimize, Scale Winners (200-500 customers)
- Month 3: Aggressive Growth (500-1,000 customers)

**Key Difference:** We'll make decisions based on REAL data, not assumptions. We'll pause what doesn't work and double down on what does.

## Risk Mitigation

### What If Month 1 Fails?

**Scenario:** Only acquire 100 customers (50% of target)

**Actions:**
1. **Analyze why:** CAC too high? Conversion too low? Product issues?
2. **Fix fundamentals:** Improve onboarding, fix bugs, address feedback
3. **Pivot strategy:** Try different channels, messaging, targeting
4. **Extend Month 1:** Spend Month 2 fixing Month 1 issues
5. **Lower Month 2 target:** Adjust to 250 customers (not 500)

**Budget Adjustment:**
- Month 1: 216K KES (planned)
- Actual: 200K KES spent
- Month 2: 200K KES (not 390K - pause, fix, then scale)

### What If CAC Is Too High?

**Scenario:** CAC stuck at 2,000+ KES

**Actions:**
1. **Audit ad creative:** Not compelling? Wrong audience?
2. **Improve landing page:** Faster load, clearer CTA?
3. **Strengthen offer:** Bigger discount? Better bonus?
4. **Focus on referrals:** Lowest CAC channel
5. **Focus on partnerships**: Free/low CAC
6. **Pause paid ads:** Until economics improve

### What If Retention Is Low?

**Scenario:** 30-day retention only 20%

**Actions:**
1. **Survey churned customers:** Why did they leave?
2. **Improve onboarding:** Make it easier to book first job
3. **Improve matching:** Better technician quality?
4. **Add engagement:** Email, SMS, notifications
5. **Fix product issues:** Bugs? UX problems?
6. **Don't scale yet:** Fix retention before acquiring more

### What If Budget Runs Out?

**Scenario:** Only 600K KES budget, not 1.1M KES

**Actions:**
1. **Focus on organic:** SEO, content, referrals (free/cheap)
2. **Focus on partnerships:** No ad spend needed
3. **Reduce ad spend:** Only keep best-performing ads
4. **Target 500 customers:** More realistic with limited budget
5. **Extend timeline:** 4-5 months instead of 3
